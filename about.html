About Aeross Journal

Aeross Journal is a student-led multidisciplinary academic journal dedicated to encouraging curiosity, innovation, and research among students. The platform provides a space where students can publish research papers, explore scientific ideas, collaborate with others, and share knowledge across multiple disciplines.

Our Mission

To make academic research more accessible, collaborative, and engaging for students passionate about science, engineering, technology, mathematics, and innovation.

What We Publish

We welcome research papers, technical reviews, exploratory studies, engineering projects, computational simulations, and interdisciplinary work from students around the world.

Why Aeross Journal?

Many students have innovative ideas and research projects but lack a proper platform to present them professionally. Aeross Journal aims to bridge that gap by providing a modern, accessible, and research-focused environment for young researchers and innovators.

Community & Collaboration

We believe research grows stronger through collaboration. Authors can work together on papers, connect through shared interests, and contribute to a growing scientific community.

Publication Process

Submitted papers go through a review process before publication. Accepted papers become publicly visible on the platform and are linked to author profiles.
